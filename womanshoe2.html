Adidas Women's Grand Court White

The Women's Gran Court White is a style of a low-top athletic shoe which has been manufactured by the German multinational company Adidas since 1969. The low-top shoe was originally released as a low-top version of the Pro Model basketball shoe.The design is nicknamed the "clam-toe", "shell-toe", "shell shoes", "shell tops", and "sea shells" (along with Adicolor).
